About JSS Innovations

Our Roots: Built in Kagiso

JSS Innovations was born out of a deep connection to our home. Growing up in Kagiso, Krugersdorp, we have lived the reality of our community every day. We’ve seen the challenges firsthand—the struggle of unemployment, the limited access to opportunities, and the clear need for modern, upgraded infrastructure.

We are here to take an active, meaningful role in the economic development of our township and our country.
