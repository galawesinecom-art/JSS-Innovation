WHO WE ARE

A Construction Company Built on Purpose

JSS Innovations (Pty) Ltd is a South African construction company founded in Kagiso, Krugersdorp, Gauteng. We specialise in construction, civil engineering, infrastructure development, renovations and project management.

Our business was established with one goal—to build infrastructure that improves lives while creating employment opportunities and contributing to sustainable economic growth throughout South Africa.

Every project we undertake reflects our commitment to quality, professionalism, safety and customer satisfaction.

OUR STORY

Built in Kagiso. Driven by Excellence.

JSS Innovations was founded on 8 July 2026 from a vision to transform construction into a vehicle for community development and economic growth. Growing up in Kagiso gave us firsthand experience of the need for improved infrastructure, better public facilities and greater employment opportunities. Rather than accepting these challenges, we chose to become part of the solution. Today, JSS Innovations is committed to delivering professional construction services while investing in local talent, building lasting relationships and contributing to South Africa's development.
